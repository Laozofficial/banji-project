Who We Are...

Welcome to IndigoGeoTech.io

IGT is a firm that consults on geographic information and provides extensive GIS services as well as vehicle tracking solutions. We serve as a problem solver by offering our clients the best GIS and GPS services both domestically and internationally. We have the support of a highly intelligent, skilled, and selfless workforce in the geoscience industry. Our history in location intelligence, vehicle tracking, and working with various GIS data/software is more than sufficient.

The corporation is eager to structure a firm with professionals and enough resources to provide a wide range of value-added services to create a long-lasting organization to achieve our desired goal.

IGT, as usual, will focus on giving our diverse client base access to cutting-edge technology that will make moving around smoother. We also use GIS technology to solve problems in a variety of areas, including public health, geology, urban planning, banking, agriculture, and other fields.
